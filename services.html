Accessibility Audit

I will review your website or application to ensure it is accessible to all users with clear, practical recommendations you can implement. I will provide a detailed report with recommendations for improvement. This includes testing for:

  • Keyboard navigation and focus management
  • Screen reader compatibility with NVDA, Jaws, and VoiceOver
  • Form validation and error handling
  • Dynamic content and ARIA implementation
