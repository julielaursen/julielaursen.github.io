My accessibility testing process

I test using Lighthouse, VoiceOver, WAVE, and NVDIA to ensure compliance with WCAG 2.1 AA standards. I prioritize the bugs I find based on the severity of the issue and the impact on the user experience. I also provide a detailed report with clear, actionable recommendations for improvement, tailored for non-technical stakeholders.
